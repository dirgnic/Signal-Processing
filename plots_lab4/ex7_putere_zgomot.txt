EXERCITIUL 7: Calculul puterii zgomotului
============================================================

Date:
  Puterea semnalului: P_signal = 90 dB
  Raport semnal-zgomot: SNR = 80 dB

Formula: SNR = P_signal - P_noise

Calcul:
  P_noise = P_signal - SNR
  P_noise = 90 dB - 80 dB
  P_noise = 10 dB
